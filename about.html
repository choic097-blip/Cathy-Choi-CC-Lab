Who I Am

Hello! I’m Cathy Choi. I’m a Korean-American creator from Rhode Island, currently a Game Design major at Parsons School of Design, I am also a webtoon artist. My passion lies in designing compelling characters and constructing intricate, original worlds, with a focus on translating those narratives into immersive experiences..

Core Philosophy

My work goes beyond simple play; I aim to design meaningful experiences. By focusing on rich character development and interactive narratives, I strive to create immersive worlds where every detail serves to pull the player deeper into the story..
