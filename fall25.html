06. Data Portrait: Drinks & Caffeine

A project that records my data over a week and expresses it as a self-portrait without visuals. When a day of the week is entered, it visually shows the caffeine intake level for that day.

07. Autobiographical Game

An autobiographical game project created using p5js. I sought to convey, in a simple yet powerful way, how an individual can transform into a bland, colorless, gray existence when consumed by self-censorship and an obsession with conformity.
