[Interactive Typography]

A collection of three generative sketches exploring the intersection of type, physics, and user interaction through code.

01. Wind

Perlin noise creates a flow field where particles form 'Wind'. Moving the mouse disturbs the letters, and they slowly return home, visualizing the fluid nature of air and movement.

02. Fishes

Text-to-points particles cling to the word 'Fishes' like a school of fish. They soft-follow the mouse with elegant trails, reacting with a soft, organic vibration that mimics marine life.

03. Heavy

The word 'Heavy' experiences strong gravitational force. Moving the mouse lifts the particles, and when they fall back, they hit the ground with an impactful screen-shaking effect.
