Project 1
Lost & Found

A p5.js generative artwork inspired by interior spaces and warm reflected light.

Description

A room with kitchen and a balcony. Outside the balcony, there are mountains covered in snow. Trees downstairs and bicycles aligned next to each other. My interpretation emphasizes a singular emotional moment: “Sunsetting Kitchen with window.”

The coding stage reduced the image even further, removing recognizable detail in favor of method and style. This shift demonstrates how interpretation often narrows from the richness of lived observation to the essentialized language of design.
