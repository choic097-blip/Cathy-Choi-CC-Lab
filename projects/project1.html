PROJECT:
LOST_&_FOUND

A p5.js generative artwork inspired by interior spaces, framed landscapes, and warm reflected light.

VISUAL_EXPERIMENT_01

MISSION_LOG: INTERPRETATION

A room with kitchen and a balcony. Outside the balcony, there are mountains covered in snow. Trees downstairs and bicycles aligned next to each other. In the middle of the room there are tables and chairs, there are three sides of windows, each window reveals a different scenery.

I interpreted the image in a more simplified, atmospheric way. Instead of focusing on the full inventory of objects, I distilled it down to a mood: “Sunsetting Kitchen with window.”

Across the stages, the image progressively became simpler and more abstract. What begins as an abundance of detail is ultimately distilled into a minimal, abstract outcome.
