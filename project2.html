Lost & Found

A p5.js generative artwork inspired by interior spaces, framed landscapes, and warm reflected light.

Description

I started with the idea of a squishy smiley face, inspired both by my own sketches and by the example Ms. Myles showed us in class. I wanted the interaction to feel playful, so I added mouse input and color changes. I also thought it would be more fun if the expression itself could shift in real time, instead of staying static. Drawing from what I learned in my Unity class, I decided to use delta time to make the movements smoother and more fluidâ€”rather than frantic or random, the transitions would feel organic, almost as if the character were breathing. Through sketching and iteration, I explored how different expressions could be exaggerated or simplified, and how these visual changes could be tied to user interaction. My goal was to strike a balance between simplicity and liveliness, so the design remains clear but also has personality.
